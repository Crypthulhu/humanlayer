Privacy Policy

Clear visibility into what data we collect, why it matters, and how we protect it end-to-end.

Last updated: February 2026 humanlayer@in6.ai

HumanLayer Systems Inc. ("we", "our", "HumanLayer") is committed to protecting the privacy of users of our platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Data collected

We collect the following information:

  • Identity information: Name, first name, professional email address
  • Professional information: Title, qualifications, authorizations, jurisdictions
  • Decision data: Decisions rendered, justifications, timestamps
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, access logs

2. Use of data

Your data is used to:

  • Verify your professional qualifications and authorizations
  • Route decision requests to qualified Sentinels
  • Document and timestamp decisions for audit trails
  • Calculate and pay compensation
  • Improve our services and prevent fraud

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide our services
  • Legal obligations: Retention of audit trails, regulatory compliance
  • Legitimate interests: Security, fraud prevention, service improvement

4. Data sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • API clients: Limited information required for decision traceability (Sentinel ID, relevant qualifications)
  • Processors: Hosting providers, payment processors (under confidentiality agreements)
  • Authorities: Only upon valid legal request

5. Data retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of the contractual relationship + 5 years
  • Audit trails: Retained for at least 10 years (legal requirements)
  • Technical data: Maximum of 12 months

6. Your rights

In accordance with GDPR and Quebec Law 25, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion (subject to legal obligations)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing

To exercise these rights: humanlayer@in6.ai

7. Cookies

We use cookies essential to the operation of the platform. No advertising or third-party tracking cookies are used.

8. International transfers

Your data may be processed in Canada and the United States. Appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses) govern any transfer outside the EU/Quebec.

9. Contact

For questions regarding this policy:
Email: humanlayer@in6.ai
Data protection officer: humanlayer@in6.ai
