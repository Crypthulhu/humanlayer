The 3 validation levels

Each level maps to a type of responsibility and expertise

Compliance validation

Structured checks of rules and policies.

  • KYC/AML
  • GDPR
  • Spend approval
  • Internal policy compliance
Value: speed + accountability + audit
2

Contextual judgment

When rules aren't enough, an expert decides.

Examples
  • Is a contract clause acceptable?
  • Process exception?
  • An anomaly requiring escalation?
Value: you buy judgment, not time
3

Decision authority

Final act reserved for an authorized person.

Examples
  • Qualified signature
  • Credit decision
  • Medical validation
  • Regulatory approval
Value: assumed accountability + strong barrier to entry
