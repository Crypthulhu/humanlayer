What it means to be a HumanLayer decision maker

Being a HumanLayer decision maker is not consulting.
It's not executing tasks.
It's not "helping an AI."

It is:

  • intervening only when a decision carries human accountability
  • signing, approving, or rejecting based on AI-prepared context
  • leaving an auditable trace of your professional judgment
  • being paid for the decision — not for time spent
